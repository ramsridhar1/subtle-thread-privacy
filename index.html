Privacy Policy – Subtle Thread

Subtle Thread does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

All thoughts, tags, and connections created in the app are stored locally on your device or synced privately via your personal iCloud account using Apple’s CloudKit framework. No data is transmitted to external servers or third-party services.

The app does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs.

If you have any questions about privacy, please contact subtlethreadapp@gmail.com.
